BAR Ø

A PODCAST COMPILATION

BAR Ø is a collection of podcasts produced by the sophomore Interaction Design students of ArtEZ. The class Sound Design is conducted by Natalia Dominguez Rangel.
The four podcasts were produces throughout the first semester of 2020.
